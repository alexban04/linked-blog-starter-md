# Gravemark

---

Это очень тяжёлый двуручный клинок, тёмный, по форме напоминает могильную плиту, покрытый цепями, трещинами.

---

**Heavy Guard** - **Малый Дар**
You gain a **+1 item bonus to Athletics checks made to Shove, Trip, or Reposition** while wielding Gravemark.

**Stand your Ground** - **Малый Дар**
==MANIPULATE==
**Активация** `[one-action]`; **Частота** Once per minute; **Эффект** - You gain a **+2 circumstance bonus to AC**.
- You cannot be **forced to move** by enemy effects unless they critically succeed.

**Dead Advance** - **Большой Дар**
==MOVEMENT==
**Активация**`[two-actions]`; **Частота** Раз в час; **Эффект** You gain temporary hitpoints equal to half of your level, rounded down. You remove all fear conditions from yourself and can stride up to your speed.

**Brace** - **Большой Дар**
**Активация**`[one-action]`; **Частота** Раз в час; **Эффект** 
You block any damage with the blade and the chains, as they absorb any harm coming your way. You gain resistance to physical damage equal to half of your level rounded down until the end of your next turn.

**Battlefield Marker** - **Великий Дар**
==MANIPULATE==
**Активация** `[two-actions]`; **Частота** Раз в сутки; **Эффект** You slam the blade in the ground under you and leave it there as a marker. For the next minute while the blade is in the ground you and your allies, while in 20ft of the blade, get +1 status bonus to attack rolls and +1 status bonus to Will Saves. Enemies treat this emanation as difficult terrain.
